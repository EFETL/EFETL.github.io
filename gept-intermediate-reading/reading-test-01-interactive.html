ARTICLE
The Benefits of Reading Aloud

Many people believe that reading silently is the fastest way to finish a book. However, researchers have found that reading aloud offers surprising benefits, especially for young learners. When we read aloud, we use both our eyes and our ears, which helps the brain remember information more firmly.

Reading aloud also improves pronunciation and builds confidence in speaking. Teachers often notice that shy students become braver after practicing this skill. Of course, reading aloud is slower, so it may not suit every situation. Still, spending just ten minutes a day reading aloud can make a real difference over time.

全文中譯

許多人認為默讀是讀完一本書最快的方式。然而，研究人員發現朗讀能帶來令人驚訝的好處，尤其對年輕學習者而言。當我們朗讀時，會同時運用眼睛與耳朵，這有助於大腦更牢固地記住資訊。

朗讀也能改善發音，並建立口說的自信。老師常注意到，害羞的學生在練習這項技巧後變得更勇敢。當然，朗讀較慢，因此未必適合每種情況。儘管如此，每天只花十分鐘朗讀，日積月累便能帶來真正的改變。
