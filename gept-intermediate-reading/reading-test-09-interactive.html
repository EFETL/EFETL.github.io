ARTICLE
Why Neighbors Matter

In today's busy world, many people hardly know the families living next door. Yet researchers say that friendly neighbors can make our lives safer and happier. When people know one another, they are more likely to watch out for each other's homes and lend a hand during hard times.

Simple actions can make a big difference. Saying hello, sharing extra vegetables, or helping carry heavy bags all help build trust. Over time, these small kindnesses turn a street of strangers into a real community. Experts suggest starting with just one friendly greeting a day. A stronger neighborhood, they say, begins with a single smile.

全文中譯

在當今忙碌的世界裡，許多人幾乎不認識住在隔壁的家庭。然而研究人員指出，友善的鄰居能讓我們的生活更安全、更快樂。當人們彼此認識，就更可能互相看顧住家，並在困難時伸出援手。

簡單的舉動就能帶來很大的不同。打招呼、分享多餘的蔬菜，或幫忙提重物，都有助於建立信任。久而久之，這些小小的善意能把一條滿是陌生人的街道，變成真正的社區。專家建議，就從每天一句友善的問候開始。他們說，更緊密的社區始於一個微笑。
