ARTICLE
Why Mistakes Help Us Learn

Many students feel embarrassed when they make mistakes. Yet researchers say that mistakes are one of the most powerful tools for learning. When we get something wrong and then correct it, our brains form stronger memories than when we simply memorize the right answer.

The key is how we react to errors. Students who see mistakes as chances to grow tend to keep trying, while those who fear failure often give up too soon. Good teachers create a safe classroom where asking questions and making mistakes are welcomed. In such places, learning feels less stressful and far more enjoyable.

全文中譯

許多學生犯錯時會感到尷尬。然而研究人員指出，錯誤是最強大的學習工具之一。當我們做錯某件事再加以更正時，大腦所形成的記憶會比單純背下正確答案時更牢固。

關鍵在於我們如何面對錯誤。把錯誤視為成長機會的學生往往會持續嘗試，而害怕失敗的人則常太早放棄。好的老師會營造一個安全的教室，讓提問與犯錯都受到歡迎。在這樣的地方，學習較不那麼有壓力，也愉快得多。
