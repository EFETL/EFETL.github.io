ARTICLE
Managing Your Time at Work

Many workers feel busy all day yet finish very little. The reason is often poor time management rather than a lack of effort. One useful habit is to list your tasks each morning and mark the two or three that matter most. Doing these first ensures that your energy goes where it counts.

Another tip is to avoid checking messages every few minutes. Constant interruptions break your focus and waste time. Instead, set fixed moments to reply. With a clear plan and fewer distractions, most people can finish their work sooner and still leave the office feeling calm.
