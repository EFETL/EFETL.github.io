ARTICLE
Why Bees Matter

Bees may be small, but they play a huge role in nature. As they move from flower to flower collecting food, they carry pollen that helps plants produce fruits and seeds. In fact, about one-third of the food we eat depends on bees and other insects.

Sadly, bee numbers have been falling in recent years. Scientists believe that chemicals used on farms and the loss of wild flowers are two major causes. To help, some people plant flowers in their gardens and avoid using harmful sprays. Protecting bees is not only about saving insects — it is about protecting our own food supply.

全文中譯

蜜蜂雖然渺小，卻在大自然中扮演巨大的角色。當牠們在花朵間採集食物、飛來飛去時，會攜帶花粉，幫助植物結出果實與種子。事實上，我們所吃的食物大約有三分之一仰賴蜜蜂與其他昆蟲。

可惜的是，近年來蜜蜂的數量一直在下降。科學家認為，農場使用的化學藥劑與野花的消失是兩大原因。為了幫忙，有些人在自家花園種花，並避免使用有害的噴霧。保護蜜蜂不只是拯救昆蟲，更是保護我們自己的糧食來源。
