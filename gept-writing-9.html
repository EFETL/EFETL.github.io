第一部分

單句寫作 Sentence Writing ‧ 50%

第 1 頁第 1–5 題：句子改寫 依提示改寫，寫出完整句子
例：The book is pink. → 提示：It ____ → 答案：It is pink.
第 2 頁第 6–10 題：句子合併 把兩句合併成一句
例：Mary has a cell phone. The cell phone is red. → 答案：Mary has a red cell phone.
第 3 頁第 11–15 題：句子重組 點字卡排出正確句子（可再點一次取消）
例：They ____. ｜ Jack / me / call → 答案：They call me Jack.

📄 參考範文（約 55 字）
Last Thursday, Tom's class went to the zoo for a school field trip. In the morning, they took a bus and left for the zoo. When they arrived, they saw many animals, such as elephants and giraffes. Tom was very happy and took many photos of them. In the evening, they took the bus home and waved goodbye to the zoo.
✅ 得分重點（自我檢查）
  • 有交代時間／人物／情境開頭（Last Thursday, Tom's class…）
  • 有寫到「搭遊覽車去動物園、看到動物」這些關鍵事件
  • 後續描述完整：看動物 → Tom 開心拍照 → 傍晚搭車回家
  • 用過去式、句子前後連貫，約 50 字
