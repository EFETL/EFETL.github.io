Lesson 3: Reading & Comprehension

Level: Intermediate 📚 Interactive Learning ⏱️ 15 minutes 📊 5 questions

Q1: Main Idea

"John loves exploring nature. Every weekend, he hikes in the mountains near his home. He believes that spending time in nature helps him relax and stay healthy. His favorite time to hike is early in the morning when the sun is rising."

What is the main idea of this passage?

Q2: Specific Detail

"Sarah is a talented musician who started playing the piano at age 5. She has won several international competitions and now teaches at a prestigious music academy. Despite her success, she still practices for 4 hours every day."

When did Sarah start playing piano?

Q3: Inference

"The store was closed on Monday. Tom drove to the shopping center, but found all the shops shut. He had traveled 30 km to get there, and the gasoline in his car was almost empty."

What can we infer from this passage?

Q4: Word Meaning (Context)

"The company's profit has surged dramatically this year, increasing by 200%. This unprecedented growth surprised even the CEO."

What does "surged" mean in this context?
